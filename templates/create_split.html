Popular Split Ideas

Push / Pull / Legs (PPL)

3-6 days. Push = chest, shoulders, triceps. Pull = back, biceps. Legs = quads, hamstrings, glutes.

Upper / Lower

4 days. Alternate between upper body and lower body days.

Full Body

3 days. Train every muscle group each session. Great for beginners.

Bro Split

5 days. One muscle group per day: chest, back, shoulders, arms, legs.

Arnold Split

6 days. Chest+Back, Shoulders+Arms, Legs — each done twice a week.
